# High-Demand, High-Paying AI Careers (2025–2026)

## Overview

This project analyzes the AI job market using 1,500 AI-related job postings from 2025–2026. The objective is to identify AI career paths that provide the strongest combination of compensation, market demand, and career growth opportunities.

Using Python and Matplotlib, the project explores salary trends, demand patterns, experience-level impacts, and AI salary premiums across a variety of AI-related careers.

---

## Research Question

**Which AI career paths offer the strongest combination of salary and market demand in the 2025–2026 job market?**

---

## Dataset

- **Dataset:** AI Jobs Market 2025–2026
- **Source:** Kaggle
- **Records:** 1,500 AI-related job postings

### Key Variables

- Job Title
- Job Category
- Annual Salary (USD)
- Demand Score
- Experience Level
- AI Salary Premium (%)

## Visualizations

Analyzes how compensation changes from entry-level to lead-level AI positions.

### 2. Top Paying AI Careers

Identifies the highest-paying AI roles in the 2025–2026 job market.

### 3. AI Career Opportunity Matrix

Compares salary and demand to identify balanced career opportunities.

### 4. AI Career Value Landscape

Combines salary, demand, and AI salary premium into a single visualization to highlight the strongest AI career opportunities.

---

## Key Findings

- AI Engineering careers provide the strongest combination of compensation and market demand.
- Senior ML Engineer, LLM Engineer, and AI Agent Developer emerged as some of the most attractive career paths.
- Specialized AI skills command higher salary premiums than general AI roles.
- Experience remains a major driver of compensation growth across AI careers.

---

## Business Implications

- Students can use these insights to prioritize skill development and career planning.
- Job seekers can target high-demand, high-paying AI career paths.
- Organizations can better understand compensation trends in the AI labor market.
